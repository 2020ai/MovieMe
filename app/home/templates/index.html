Tweet Detective

Tweet Detective is a web application that provides deep insights about the most recent tweets containing the search terms user desires. It identifies and plots the most popular hashtags. Sentiment analysis is performed to find the sentiment score of each tweet and displaying the most positive and the most negative tweets found, followed by topic modeling in order to highlight the hot topics discussed. The most important words for each identified topic are illustrated.

Please submit your search term(s) below

For instance, you can search for Walmart vs Costco, RBC vs Scotia Bank, McDonald vs Tim Hortons, or your favorite Netflix movies and shows.
